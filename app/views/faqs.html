Frequently Asked Questions

The purpose of this page is to answer any questions you might have about the ensemble. If there is a question that isn't answered here, feel free to contact us!

What do you guys do, exactly?

We compete annually in the Winter Guard International (WGI) circuit.

We put together a show that is approximately 6-8 minutes long, and spend about 5 months perfecting it musically, and visually.

How much money does it cost?

That is a good question. I'm not sure...50?

How old do I have to be to do this?

Most of our students are in High School and College, but, we have had some successful members that were in Junior High, too!
